Y Combinator

Y Combinator is a well-known startup accelerator that helps early-stage companies grow by providing funding, mentorship, and resources.

Startups join a short program where they build and improve their product, then present it to investors on “Demo Day” to raise more funding.

It is one of the most recognized startup accelerators and has helped launch many successful companies.
