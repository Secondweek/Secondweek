Techstars

Techstars is one of the best-known startup accelerators in the world.

It offers startups access to mentors, alumni, investors, and a strong support network. It runs many programs across industries such as fintech, healthtech, and other areas.

Techstars is often seen as a strong choice for founders who want both guidance and networking opportunities.
